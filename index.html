UNDER THE RUG CLEANERS

UTRC is a corporation with the main motive of disposing and recycling of e-waste. In present-day India, there is a huge problem regarding wastage. To tackle this problem, India has managed to create an many infrastructures, but there is a lack of service to tie all these infrastructures. Which prevents the general public from disposing e-waste easily.

Our corporation aims to fix that problem. We plan to partner with E-Recyclers from Government and Corporate sector all over India to help citizens provide ease of access to all their services.
